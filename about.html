History

Crypto Sabers was created with one purpose, to allow the people of the world to have their imaginations come true with the creation of their very own, high quality, lightsaber. Founders Tim Dietrich and Robbie Burton were once wee lads and were playing with their store-bought toy lightsabers and Tim thought to himself, "Why do we have this garbage plastic when we can just make the real thing?". Over a course of 20 years, the pair we coming up with ideas until they landed on Crypto Sabers.
All custom lightsabers are built by hand in Wales and shipping is worldwide and takes generally 2-3 weeks to ship. All purchases come with a 100% money-back gurantee return policy which lasts 90 days. The return address is located on the packaging.
